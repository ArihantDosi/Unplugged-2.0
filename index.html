About the Event-

UNPLUGGED 2.0 is the premier hardware hackathon event. Join us for a weekend of innovation, collaboration, and hands-on hardware hacking!

Workshops & Sessions-

Dive into a series of hands-on workshops and insightful sessions led by industry experts.
